Seating plan special
======================

The **Special** seating map page creates room plans by clicking directly on a room image.

Three numbering options are available:

- Numbers
- Letters
- Prefix

First choose a room plan, then choose the numbering mode. Click the image to place each label.

The number option starts at 1 and increments by one. The letters option starts at A and continues with AA, AB, and so on. The prefix option adds a custom prefix before the number, for example ``A-1`` or ``F-2``.

Use **Undo** to remove the last label. If the numbering option or prefix changes, numbering restarts at 1.
