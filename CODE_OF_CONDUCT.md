# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using inclusive and respectful language
- Being open to constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Public or private harassment

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and taking corrective action.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to:

**cepro-exams@epfl.ch**

All complaints will be reviewed fairly.
